Blueberry Pie Recipe

A delicious homemade blueberry pie with a flaky crust.

Ingredients

  • 2 pie crusts (homemade or store-bought)
  • 5 cups fresh blueberries, washed and drained
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • Egg wash (1 egg beaten with 1 tablespoon water)
  • Granulated sugar, for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out one pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the blueberries, granulated sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, cinnamon, and salt. Stir until the blueberries are evenly coated.
  4. Pour the blueberry filling into the prepared pie crust.
  5. Dot the filling with pieces of butter.
  6. Roll out the second pie crust and place it over the blueberry filling. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal.
  7. Brush the top crust with egg wash and sprinkle with granulated sugar.
  8. Make several small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  9. Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  10. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack before serving.
